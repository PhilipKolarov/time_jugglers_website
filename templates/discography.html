A Fraction of Consciousness (EP)

  • Year: 2023
  • Length: 18:13
  • Tracks: 4
  • Recorded & Mixed: Vibesbuilder Studios
  • Mastered: Urban House Studio

Recorded in Germany in May 2022, this extended play develops the sound of "The Tyrant Within", but taking it into a completely different dimension. The concept this time explores how two sides of one's mind face each other and try to settle their differences.

The Tyrant Within (LP)

  • Year: 2022
  • Length: 48:58
  • Tracks: 13
  • Recorded, Mixed & Mastered: Urban House Studio

Our 2nd studio album marked a slight change in direction. This time we began incorporating more modern sounds and focused more on the groovier elements. "The Tyrant Within" is also our first concept record, examining how difficult experiences and bad decisions can bring an individual to their knees.

Stories in Time (LP)

  • Year: 2019
  • Length: 34:32
  • Tracks: 8
  • Recorded: Sound City Studios
  • Mixed & Mastered: Vasil Raykov

Our very first full length album was a collection of tracks written over our first three years as a group (hence the name). As a result, the tracks' styles range from blues and soft rock to classic and speed metal.
